John A. Kufuor Foundation

Continuing the Legacy of Leadership and Service

Established in 2011, the Foundation advances President Kufuor's lifelong commitment to ethical leadership, public health, nutrition, and sustainable development across Africa.

Our Programs Support Our Work

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To promote good governance, leadership development, health improvement, and sustainable development across Africa through evidence-based programs and strategic partnerships.

Our Vision

An Africa where ethical leadership, good health, and sustainable development create prosperity and dignity for all people.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, Collaboration, and Commitment to serving humanity with dignity and respect.

Our Core Programs

Leadership Development Institute

Empowering Africa's Next Generation of Leaders

  • Young Leaders Program: Annual fellowship for emerging African leaders aged 25-35
  • Women in Leadership: Specialized training for women in governance and public service
  • Executive Education: Advanced courses for senior government officials and civil servants
  • Leadership Mentorship: One-on-one mentoring with experienced African leaders

Health & Nutrition Initiative

Building Healthier Communities Across Africa

  • Maternal Health Program: Reducing maternal mortality through improved healthcare access
  • Child Nutrition: Combating malnutrition through school feeding and community programs
  • Healthcare Infrastructure: Supporting the development of rural health facilities
  • Health Policy Advocacy: Promoting universal health coverage across Africa
